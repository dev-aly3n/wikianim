Kaji is one of the most well-known voice actors in Japan, having voiced characters such as Eren Yeager in Attack on Titan, Shoto Todoroki in My Hero Academia, and many more. He has won several awards, including Best Voice Actor at the 12th Tokyo Anime Awards (2013), Best Actor at the 7th Seiyu Awards (2013), and Best Voice Actor at the 36th Anime Grand Prix (2014).

\n

Married voice actress Ayana Taketatsu in 2019.
